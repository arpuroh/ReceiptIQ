Intelligent Technology for Seamless Grocery Management

Our Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Upload & Pre-process: Your receipt image undergoes pre-processing (grayscale, de-skew, crop) using OpenCV.js in a browser web-worker for optimal clarity.
  2. Accurate OCR: Tesseract.js Workers run in parallel for fast, on-device Optical Character Recognition. For challenging receipts, AWS Textract serves as a powerful fallback if confidence is below 85%.
  3. Smart Parsing: We use a combination of Regular Expressions (Regex) and price heuristics to accurately parse line items, extracting details like item name, quantity, and price.
  4. Item Disambiguation: Advanced LLMs (like Gemini Vision or specific OpenAI models) clarify and normalize item names, ensuring consistency.
  5. Enrichment: Item data is enriched with categories (e.g., “Produce > Vegetables”) and nutritional information (calories, macros) by referencing taxonomies and the USDA FoodData Central API.
  6. Bank Linking: Receipts are intelligently matched to bank transactions from your Plaid feed using a fuzzy logic approach on timestamp (±2 days) and amount (±$0.50).
  7. Secure Storage: Processed data (JSON rows) is stored securely in our Postgres database. Original images are only stored in an S3-compatible vault if you explicitly opt-in to cloud archive.

Simplified System Architecture:

Client-Side Power:

Our React-based Web Client handles uploads. A Web Worker performs on-device OCR, ensuring your data can be processed privately.

Intelligent Backend:

An NER (Named Entity Recognition) / LLM Categorizer API processes the extracted data. This data is then stored efficiently in a Postgres / Supabase database.

Secure Bank Sync:

Our Bank Match Engine securely communicates with Personal Finance APIs like Plaid to link your receipts with transactions.

Flexible Data Output:

Access your data through CSV exports or integrate with other services via Webhooks (coming soon).

Your Data, Your Control. Privacy First, Always.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We believe in privacy-first defaults and giving you full control over your information. Here's how we protect your data:

  • On-Device OCR Processing: Whenever possible, receipt images are processed directly on your device using WebAssembly Tesseract. This means your sensitive receipt data doesn't need to leave your computer or phone for initial processing.
  • Immediate Image Purge (Default): After successful JSON data extraction, the original receipt image is immediately purged from our systems by default, unless you specifically enable our "Cloud Archive" feature.
  • Secure Cloud Archive (Optional): If you choose to use the Cloud Archive feature (coming in Phase III), your receipt images will be stored with strong AES-256 server-side encryption in a secure S3-compatible vault. You control this setting.

Secure Data Handling & Bank Connections

  • Bank Credentials: We use Plaid for bank connections. Plaid tokenizes your bank credentials, meaning ReceiptIQ never stores or even sees your raw login information.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and store the data necessary to provide our services.
  • Data Export & Erasure: You have the right to export your data and request erasure in line with GDPR & CCPA regulations. Tools for this will be available in Phase III.

Robust Security Measures

  • SOC-2 Pathway: We are committed to security best practices and are on a pathway towards SOC-2 compliance, including comprehensive logging (OpenTelemetry → Loki/Grafana) and quarterly vulnerability scans.
  • COPPA Compliance: We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Sign-up includes DOB gating to prevent underage registration.
  • PII Protection: We plan to implement auto-redaction of sensitive PII like loyalty barcodes if you opt out of cloud storage.
  • Regular Audits: We will conduct regular security audits and penetration testing (e.g., via Bugcrowd VTDP pre-launch) to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
